Remuneration Committee
 e Remuneration Committee comprises Roger Knowles and Norman Cave and is chaired by Roger Knowles.  e Chief Executive also attends meetings by
invitation.
Executive remuneration policy and structure
 e Group's aim is to provide su cient levels of remuneration to attract, retain and motivate Executive Directors, but to avoid paying more than is necessary for
this purpose. Remuneration packages are structured so as to link rewards to corporate and individual performance and they incorporate a combination of fixed
and variable elements. 
 e table includes a summary of the remuneration of the Directors. 
Share schemes
 e Company operates a number of share schemes and these are detailed below. Details of the Executive Directors' participation in these schemes are set out 
below.
Sharesave scheme
Invitations are periodically made to eligible employees to apply for options under the scheme to be paid for by way of monthly contributions from the employee's
post-tax salary.
Directors' contracts
All Executive Directors have rolling contracts with 12-month notice provisions.
Non-Executive Directors
Non-Executive Directors are paid annual fees set by the Board. Roger Knowles and Norman Cave have rolling contracts determinable on either side by giving
three months' notice. Patrick Lineen, as part-time Financial Director, has a rolling contract with a 12-month notice provision.
