SB Chemicals
SB Chemicals, a manufacturer and supplier of cleaning products based in Lisburn, Northern Ireland, was acquired in October 2004 for a gross consideration of  1.43 million. In the last 10 months we have transferred the manufacture of over 95% of the Group's liquid chemical cleaning product requirements from third party suppliers to SB Chemicals. This transfer was not without its challenges, but I am pleased to report that the transfer was a success and has contributed to the increase in our gross profit margin to 50% (2004: 46%). Our manufacturing plant in Lisburn now produces some125 product lines and operates at a run rate of 8 million litres of product per annum. The Group can double its production at this facility with very little additional capital expenditure.
Additionally we have successfully uplifted the accounting software onto the Group's preferred accounting platform. We are confident that during this financial year SB Chemicals' contribution to Group profits will increase.
